Appendix - How the Formulas Work

Bonus Scheme

Payout = Bonus % x (Annual Salary / 12)

Where Bonus % is looked up from:
0% margin -> 0% of month salary
5% -> 20% | 10% -> 45% | 15% -> 70%
20% -> 100% | 25% -> 120% | 30% -> 140%
35% -> 160% | 40% -> 180% | 45% -> 200%
50% -> 220%

Scales with individual salary

Profit Sharing

Payout = (Margin - 10%) x Revenue / 2 / Team Size

Only kicks in when profitability > 10%.
Take the excess profit above the 10% margin,
split 50/50 (company keeps half),
divide the team's half equally.

Same for everyone regardless of salary
Spreadsheet note: The spreadsheet uses 10% x Revenue for the retained amount (Row 9 references Row 2). This is algebraically identical to "profit above the 10% margin" since: Profit - 10% x Revenue = Revenue x (Margin - 10%).
